Lasagna

This delicious italian dish will make your family go nuts!

You can make is veggy too!

Ingredients

  • Lasagna Pasta: 6 sheets per serving
  • Tomato Sauce
  • Ground Beef
  • 2 Onions
  • 2 Carrots
  • 2 Red Peppers

Preparation

Sauce

Cut all the vegetables into small pieces using the Juliana cut, and fry then in a hot pan with a good splash of olive oil. Add two smashed garlic gloves, some salt and pepper, and finally a small amount of white wine

Upon caramelization starts, add the ground beef while chopping the meat using a wooden spoon.
